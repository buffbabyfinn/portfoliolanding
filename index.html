About me!

I am 25 years old, and I've lived in Oregon for the last 4 of them. I went to community college in Illinois for English, but moved out west before I finished my degree. I've been working as a barista at a few coffee roasteries for the last 8 years. I've been playing around on computers and video game consoles my entire life, my father is a computer engineer. Partially because I need a change of career and partially because of the above reasons I choose now to pursue software development. I am really excited about mobile gaming, and I think that it is where the future of gaming lies. I would love to be a part of the indie android gaming industry in the future. Outside of computer, console, and mobile gaming, I play several instruments. Including, but not limited to ukulele, hammered dulcimer, bass, and clarinet. I spend a lot of time checking out the local music scene. Portland has got some really amazing artists these days. I am also a baker professionally and a knitter recreationally. And, after eight years in the coffee industry, I can make a pretty mean cup of coffee.
